Our Beginnings

JCA Records was founded in 2013 by Joel Perez, Armond Chatman and Chris Coakley. The three met in high school, and became friends bonded by a common passion -- music. Unfortunetly, music couldn't save them from stirring up mischef, and they were expelled from Schenectady High School after a prank on staff memebers went wrong in 2011. For more than two years, the three friends continued to hang out and work dead-end jobs, but all wanted more out of life. Armond was an aspiring musician with a decent local following, but couldn't garner the attention of mid-sized or even small record labels. Joel and Chris knew quite a bit about the music and entertainment industry, as Joel was the son of singer and actress Rosie Perez, and Chris's uncle Tim Coakley was a well-known jazz drummer and enthusiast. Wanting to help Armond reach his goals and make something of their own lives, Chris and Joel decided they wanted to start a record label. However, when they approached Armond with the idea, he didn't want their help. He wanted in. So with that, JCA Records -- or at least the concept -- was born. Today, JCA Records is one of the top small labels in New York State, and continue to help local artists reach their full potential.

Our Founders

Joel Perez - Director of Label Operations

Joel Perez was born in the manhattan neigbourhood of washington heights , growing up he always had an ear for percussion instruments and handmade beats his mother, curated his love for music in middle school and that found it's way to him to this day through his craft as director of label operations.

Chris Coakley - Head of Artist Development

A native of Latham, Chris grew up playing guitar and writing songs for fun. At first, he aspired to be a famous musician, but then realized that he didn't like playing in front of people.

Armond Chatman - Lead Audio Engineer

Armond Chatman was born in Schenectady ny a local native to the underground music scene as a teen he found a passionate interest in audio engineering and decided to pursue his dreams in that particular field through our record label.
