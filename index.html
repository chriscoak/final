NYS approves grant for young artist program

JCA Records is pleased to annouce that they have recieved a $50,000 grant from the Board of Education to fund Startists, their new artist development program for high school students. The program, which would take place twice a week during after school hours, would allow students to experience the life of a music industry professional. Experinces would include recording and editing audio for JCA clients, attending performances, scouting talent, and shadowing meetings with JCA representatives.
